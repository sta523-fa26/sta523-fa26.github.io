classes end Tuesday, November 24, 2026 and do not resume after the Thanksgiving
recess. The class meeting dates are therefore 08/24 - 11/24.

## Notes

- Fall Break begins Friday, Oct 9 at 7:00 PM, so the daytime Friday lab on Oct 9
  still meets; classes resume Wednesday, Oct 14. Only the Monday Oct 12 lecture is lost.
- Thanksgiving recess begins Tuesday, Nov 24 at 10:30 PM. Because this is a graduate
  course, classes end Nov 24 and do not resume, so the last class meeting is
  Monday, November 23, 2026. (Undergraduate sections resume Nov 30 and run through
  Dec 4, but that does not apply here.)
- Founders' Weekend (Sept 24-27) does not cancel any classes; classes are held
  Thursday and Friday.
- Duke does not observe Veterans Day (Wed, Nov 11), so class meets as usual.
